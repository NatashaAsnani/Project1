OUR STORY

We are a little retro motel in the Okanagan undergoing some TLC. We joke that we are a Mo-Hotel, combining the affordability of a motel with the 5-star service of a hotel. We believe everyone deserves a 5-star experience (just like Beyonce) and hope our little thoughtful touches make you feel like a pop star! Now let's get to know us!

The Lakeside Villa is a passion project being created by me – Julie Ziola Dery I am a CPA by day and an interior designer by night. I have always wanted to combine my skill of numbers and passion of pretty things into something exciting. This little motel was the perfect fit! I am a bonified DIY enthusiast with an obsession for upcycling, so if I can save something from going to a landfill and repurpose it you better believe that I will. Oh yah, and I also love wine! So having this little gem in the heart of wine country isn’t too shabby. Please stop by and say hi to me when you visit our property as I would love to meet you. I am honored you are going to choose us (you are ... right?) We will do our best to help you create lasting memories with friends and family. 3 fun facts about me:

1. I cold plunge every Sunday.

2. I am obsessed with cooking shows – especially ‘Beat Bobby Flay.’

3. I am constantly listening to crime podcasts so please don’t sneak up on me.
