ACTIVITIES

You will run out of time before you run out of things to see and do!

The activities immediately surrounding Lakeside Villa are bountiful and you will want to plan to ensure you make the most of your vacation retreat starting at our motel. Restaurants, golf, wineries, cycling, water fun and winter recreation are within a short walk or drive.

RESTAURANTS

Sonneto at Play

Sonetto Italian Restaurant, embraces the opportunity to both entertain and educate while providing a genuine customer experience. Their goal is to create a mini escape from day-to-day life while highlighting exquisite Italian cuisine, extraordinary wines, and a majestic, unobstructed, world class view of the Skaha Lake and surrounding mountains.

GOLF

Skaha Meadows Golf Course

It is only a 5 minute walk to Skaha Meadows Golf Course and is renowned in the Okanagan area for its friendly atmosphere. With six par 4's and one par 5, the 2,435 yard, 9-hole layout is both challenging and rewarding. The natural meadow setting with native grasses and well-maintained, tree-lined fairways makes playing Skaha Meadows a great course for all players.

CYCLING

Kettle Valley Rail (KVR) Trail

Located next to the Lakeside Villa the most renowned location for leisure cycling in Penticton is the Kettle Valley Rail (KVR) Trail. Join the Kettle Valley Rail Trail and head south along the shore of Skaha Lake. You’ll pass through the village of Kaleden. In Okanagan Falls, you have another good option to ride down to Vaseux Lake along the historic KVR Trail.

Motor Bikes

Is there a better way to tour Okanagan wine country? Rent an electric bicycle and take a self-guided tour with your BFFs. Sit back, relax, take in the flavours, soak up the scenery, and enjoy the E-Kruise experience. Located next door to the Lakeside Villa you can book in advance on line to have your e-bike ready for you day of riding.

WATER FUN

Swimming Pool in Skaha Lake

Access to this private gated swimming pool is available to all guests of Lakeside Villa Inn & Suites. A short two-minute walk in a passage way under the road provides safe access to the best beach in Penticton. Private washrooms, fire pits and picnic tables are also available for motel guests.

Beach Boats

One of Penticton’s top bucket list items on a hot summer day is to lie back on a rubber floatie and let the lazy river carry your stress away. The 7 km lake to lake channel float is a must do summer activity in Penticton and great fun for the whole family. The float starts at Coyote Cruises on Riverside Drive and slowly drifts from Okanagan Lake all the way down to Skaha Lake

WINTER ENTERTAINMENT

Snow Skating

Lakeside Villa Inn & Suites is only 35 minutes from Apex Mountain Resort (closest accommodation in Penticton) without having to stay on the mountain. With an average of 600 cm of annual snowfall, Apex provides the moderate winter conditions and dry powder snow craved by skiers and snowboarders alike. Apex Mountain Resort is the only mountain in the Okanagan that faces northeast, those beautiful powder snow crystals are less affected by the sun and the famous light.
